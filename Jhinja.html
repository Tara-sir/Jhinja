About Jhulkhet

Jhulkhet is a serene and beautiful village nestled in the hills of Western Rukum, one of Nepal's 77 districts. With approximately 50 houses and a population of more than 200 people, the village offers a peaceful rural life while being surprisingly well-connected to neighboring towns and cities.

This small yet lively village is a part of the Sitalpokhari-Jhulkhet-Chunbang Highway, providing an important link to the Rolpa district and beyond. Jhulkhet is located:

  • 5 km northeast of the Mid-Hill Highway
  • 5 km from the Rapti Highway
  • 12 km from Salley Airport
  • 8 km from Musikot Khalanga, the district headquarters

Despite being a small village, Jhulkhet plays a crucial role as a business hub for neighboring villages like Sankh, Chaukhabang, and Bhalakcha.
